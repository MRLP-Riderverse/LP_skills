# Recent capture patterns (June 2026)

Observed in live Acadie-Bathurst intake:

- Users often send one-line bundles with business name, street address, landmark, phone, and hours all at once.
- Good inbox capture preserves the original landmark relation in Notes rather than forcing separate structured fields too early.
- Examples of useful landmark phrasing:
  - `within Place Bathurst Mall`
  - `next to Place Bathurst Mall`
  - `after the Bowlarama and Big D Drive In`
- Phone, hours, and descriptive food/service blurbs can remain in Notes at draft stage.
- The short confirmation after capture should stay lean: normalized title plus any explicit top-level location/area that was captured.

Use this file as a compact reminder when staging new local business drafts.
