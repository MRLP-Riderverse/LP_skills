# Music Industry Research Task

Monitor music industry updates with focus on:
- Specific artist interests: Green Day, Bring Me The Horizon (BMTH), Jaden, Joji
- Music distribution platforms and innovations
- Web3 music adoption (Audius, Royal, Sound.xyz, etc.)
- Creator ecommerce (Fourthwall, Shopify for artists, etc.)
- Music tech that actually works in practice

GOALS:
- Provide meaningful updates on followed artists
- Identify actual distribution/platform innovations
- Assess web3 music adoption beyond experiments
- Evaluate creator tools for real artist use
- Separate signal from noise in music news
- Give practical takeaways for artists and fans

FRAMEWORK:
For each area monitored, answer:
1. What is the actual development/news?
2. What problem does it address for artists/fans?
3. Who is actually using it (not just experimenting)?
4. What makes it different from previous solutions?
5. Is it usable today by real artists/fans?
6. What are the adoption barriers or limitations?
7. Is it meaningful innovation or incremental improvement?

Then provide:
- Artist-specific updates section
- Platform/distribution developments
- Web3 music adoption insights
- Creator economy trends
- Practical recommendations
- Plain-language summary of what matters

Focus on:
- What working musicians actually adopt
- What fans actually engage with
- What solves real problems in music creation/distribution
- What has sustainable momentum beyond hype
