# Music & Creator Philosophy

- Prioritize what working artists actually use over speculative future tech
- Focus on solutions that solve real problems in creation, distribution, or fan engagement
- Prefer tools with low friction for independent artists
- Value direct artist-fan connections over intermediaries when possible
- Assess sustainability: Can this model last beyond venture funding?
- Look for actual adoption metrics, not just announcements or pilot programs
- Consider the artist's perspective: Time, learning curve, cost vs benefit
- Remember that music is fundamentally about connection and expression
- Technology should serve the art, not complicate it
- Simple solutions that work often beat complex ones that promise more
- Community and authenticity matter more than technological novelty
