# Creative Content Workflows

Class-level skill for creative media and publishing workflows.

Use this when the task is about:
- generating or iterating on images
- writing or rewriting creative text for human tone
- composing or prompting music
- turning long-form media into shareable content
- publishing content to social or distribution channels

## Core workflow

1. Identify the medium and intended audience.
2. Transform the source material into the target format.
3. Keep the creative direction consistent across iterations.
4. Verify the final output in the destination context when possible.

## Subsections

### Image generation and visual synthesis

- Generate images from text prompts.
- Iterate on style, composition, and mood.
- Keep prompt intent aligned with the intended output format.

### Humanized writing

- Rewrite output so it reads natural and specific.
- Remove generic AI phrasing when the user wants a more human voice.

### Music and songwriting

- Write lyric and songwriting prompts.
- Shape mood, structure, and instrumentation.
- Keep the song brief aligned with the user's target style.

### Transcript-to-content workflows

- Convert transcripts or long media into summaries, threads, blogs, or clips.
- Preserve the important claims while compressing the rest.

### Social publishing

- Prepare platform-specific output.
- Match length, tone, and formatting to the channel.
- Verify the final post content before sending.

## Verification checklist

- Check that the output matches the requested medium.
- Check that the tone matches the audience.
- Check that platform-specific constraints are respected.
- If publishing, verify the sent payload or final posted content.

## Notes

This umbrella replaces narrow creative helpers that all boil down to media generation, rewriting, repurposing, or publishing.
